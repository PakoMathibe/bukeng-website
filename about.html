Our Story

Building a More Accessible
Food Future

Bukeng was born from a simple observation: millions of South Africans struggle with food access due to timing mismatches between needs and income. We're here to change that.

Our Mission

To democratize food access by providing fair, transparent, and flexible payment solutions that work for every South African, regardless of their income timing. We believe that no one should have to choose between buying groceries today and paying bills tomorrow.

Our Vision

A South Africa where financial constraints never stand between a person and their next meal. Where food security is a reality, not a privilege. We envision Bukeng becoming the infrastructure for food access across the continent, starting with our home, South Africa.
